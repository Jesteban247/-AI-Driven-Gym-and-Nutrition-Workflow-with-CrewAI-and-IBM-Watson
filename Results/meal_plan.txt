**Bulking Meal Plan (7 Days)**

**Day 1**

* Meal 1 (Breakfast): Scrambled eggs with whole wheat toast and avocado, 3 whole eggs, 2 slices whole wheat toast, 1/2 avocado (350 calories, 25g protein, 30g carbs, 20g fat)
* Meal 2 (Post-Workout Snack): Greek yogurt with berries and almonds, 1 cup Greek yogurt, 1 cup mixed berries, 1 oz almonds (250 calories, 20g protein, 30g carbs, 10g fat)
* Meal 3 (Lunch): Grilled chicken breast with quinoa and steamed broccoli, 4 oz grilled chicken breast, 1 cup cooked quinoa, 1 cup steamed broccoli (400 calories, 35g protein, 30g carbs, 10g fat)
* Meal 4 (Mid-Day Snack): Apple slices with peanut butter, 1 medium apple, 2 tbsp peanut butter (150 calories, 4g protein, 20g carbs, 8g fat)
* Meal 5 (Dinner): Grilled salmon with sweet potato and green beans, 4 oz grilled salmon, 1 medium sweet potato, 1 cup green beans (400 calories, 35g protein, 30g carbs, 10g fat)
* Meal 6 (Before Bed Snack): Cottage cheese with cucumber slices, 1 cup cottage cheese, 1 medium cucumber (150 calories, 20g protein, 5g carbs, 5g fat)

**Day 2**

* Meal 1 (Breakfast): Oatmeal with banana and almond butter, 1 cup cooked oatmeal, 1 medium banana, 2 tbsp almond butter (350 calories, 10g protein, 40g carbs, 15g fat)
* Meal 2 (Post-Workout Snack): Protein shake with whey protein and mixed berries, 1 scoop whey protein, 1 cup mixed berries (200 calories, 25g protein, 30g carbs, 5g fat)

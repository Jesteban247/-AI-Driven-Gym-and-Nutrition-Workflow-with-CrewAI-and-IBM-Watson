Here is a 6-day push/pull/leg gym split for hypertrophy based on scientific research:

Day 1: Push Day (Chest and Triceps)
- Barbell Bench Press: 4 sets, 6-12 reps
- Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets, 10-15 reps
- Cable Flyes: 3 sets, 12-15 reps
- Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets, 10-12 reps
- Overhead Dumbbell Extension: 3 sets, 12-15 reps
Rest time: 60-90 seconds between sets

Day 2: Pull Day (Back and Biceps)
- Pull-ups: 3 sets, 8-12 reps
- Barbell Rows: 4 sets, 8-12 reps
- Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets, 10-12 reps
- Dumbbell Bicep Curls: 3 sets, 10-12 reps
- Hammer Curls: 3 sets, 10-12 reps
Rest time: 60-90 seconds between sets

Day 3: Leg Day (Squats and Deadlifts)
- Squats: 4 sets, 8-12 reps
- Leg Press: 3 sets, 10-12 reps
- Lunges: 3 sets, 10-12 reps per leg
- Deadlifts: 4 sets, 8-12 reps
- Leg Extensions: 3 sets, 12-15 reps
Rest time: 60-90 seconds between sets

Day 4: Push Day (Shoulders and Chest)
- Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 4 sets, 8-12 reps
- Lateral Raises: 3 sets, 10-12 reps
- Rear Delt Flyes: 3 sets, 12-15 reps
- Incline Bench Press: 4 sets, 8-12 reps
- Chest Flyes: 3 sets, 12-15 reps
Rest time: 60-90 seconds between sets

Day 5: Pull Day (Back and Biceps)
- Lat Pulldowns: 4 sets, 8-12 reps
- Seated Cable Rows: 3 sets, 10-12 reps
